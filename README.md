# Hospitality-Performance-and-Revenue-Insights

1. Headline

A comprehensive Hospitality Performance Analytics Dashboard designed to monitor revenue, occupancy, and operational efficiency across hotels, cities, room classes, and booking channels. As a Data Analyst, this project transforms raw booking and revenue data into actionable insights to support pricing strategy, demand forecasting, and performance optimization.

2. Purpose

This project focuses on analyzing hotel revenue and occupancy performance using transactional booking data. The objective is to track key hospitality KPIs, identify demand patterns, and uncover revenue drivers across properties, locations, and customer booking channels through an interactive, decision-ready dashboard.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Primary visualization and reporting platform

📂 Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and shaping

🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for KPI calculations, WoW changes, and performance metrics

📝 Data Modeling – Star-schema modeling to enable efficient filtering and aggregation

📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews

4. Data Source

AtliQ Grands hotel booking and revenue data covering multiple cities, properties, room classes, booking channels, and time periods, including metrics such as Revenue, Occupancy %, ADR, RevPAR, DSRN, DBRN, and Cancellation rates.

5. Highlights
   
Business Problem: Hospitality businesses generate large volumes of booking data, but decision-makers often struggle to answer key performance questions quickly using raw reports.

Key questions such as:

* Which cities and properties are driving the most revenue?
* How are RevPAR, ADR, and Occupancy trending week-over-week?
* Which booking channels deliver higher realization and lower cancellations?
* Are weekends outperforming weekdays?
…are difficult to address without a centralized analytical view.

Goal of the Dashboard:

To build an interactive hospitality analytics dashboard that:
* Tracks revenue and operational KPIs at a property and city level
* Enables quick comparison across booking channels and room classes
* Identifies demand patterns, performance gaps, and revenue opportunities
* Supports data-driven pricing and inventory decisions

Walkthrough of Key Visuals

* Key KPIs (Top Section): Total Revenue: ~1.69B | RevPAR, ADR, Occupancy %, DSRN, and Realisation with WoW change indicators

* Revenue by Category (Donut Chart): Business vs Luxury revenue contribution, showing category-wise demand split

* Trend by Key Metrics (Line Chart): Weekly trends for RevPAR, ADR, and Occupancy % to monitor performance momentum

* Property Performance Table: Property-wise comparison of Revenue, Bookings, RevPAR, Occupancy, ADR, Cancellations, and Ratings

* Channel Analysis (Bar & Line Chart): Realisation % and ADR across booking platforms to identify high-performing channels

Business Impact & Insights

* Enabled end-to-end performance visibility across cities and properties, reducing manual reporting effort
* Identified Luxury category contributing ~62% of total revenue, supporting premium pricing strategies
* Revealed weekend occupancy outperforming weekdays, indicating demand-based pricing opportunities
* Highlighted channel-wise realization and cancellation trends, helping optimize booking channel mix
* Provided a single source of truth for revenue, occupancy, and demand planning decisions
